About Us

The Finance Club at PDPU aims to bring together all students who share an interest and passion for finance and investments and share their knowledge and insights with each other. It aims at enabling them to discover recent trends and development in the field while allowing each individual to grow personally. We aim to provide the students with a hands on experience by organizing developmental opportunities through guest speakers, panel discussions, mock stock market simulation and many more such events in the field of finance. The purpose of finance club is to promote interest and impart their knowledge in the field of finance. To become a space of learning in the field of finance and investment that creates successful professionals. Foster an environment among student to encourage continuous learning and discovery of recent trends in finance. Help members develop practical investment skills and test them in the markets; and to create superior access to career opportunities in the investment industry.
